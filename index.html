Game description

Alice Wonder Match takes you on a whimsical journey back to Wonderland!
Follow Alice as she embarks on a magical quest full of challenges and surprises.
Match dazzling gems, defeat tricky foes, and unlock powerful abilities to aid Alice on her adventure.
From the Mad Hatter’s tea party to the Queen’s castle, every level offers a new piece of the story.
Can you help Alice restore balance to Wonderland?
Your match-3 adventure begins now!
